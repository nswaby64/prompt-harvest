How it works

Three steps. That's it.

No setup. No accounts. Just open a YouTube video and let Prompt Harvest do the work.

1

Open any YouTube video

Navigate to any YouTube video that covers AI, productivity, coding, writing — or any topic with useful prompts inside.

2

Click "Show Transcript"

Expand the video description and click "Show Transcript" so YouTube loads the captions. Then open the Prompt Harvest extension.

3

Hit "Harvest Prompts"

Click the button. Claude AI scans the transcript in seconds and surfaces every usable AI prompt, neatly categorized and ready to copy.
